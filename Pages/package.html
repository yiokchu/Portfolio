TEA PACKAGING

This is the package design for a whole collection of flower teas; Jasmine Tea, Rose Tea, Lavender Tea, Hibiscus Tea, and Chamomile Tea. The collection was design to transmit a clear, clean, and calming energy.

For this project, I decided to use pastel colors to give the packages a feel of lightness, softness, and calmness. This matches the product, it gives it a feeling of earthiness and sophistication.
The collection is made from flat icons to focus on color and shape. Making the illustrations flow between packages was a decision made to give consistency, allowing the packages all to look like a unified collection.

NESTLE REDESIGN



Collaboration with Thai Van Nguyen



For this project, we consider how to assess the quality of the Nestle package design, and we made informed design decisions to redesign both the product package (the box itself) and the logo of the company.

For the redesign we considered:

  2. The client (company that makes the product) and the consumer (person or community who buys the product)
  3. The Color scheme
  4. Composition
  5. Typography
  6. Logos and branding
