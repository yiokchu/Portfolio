DecoBap

Description
DecoBap specializes in the selling of personalized
desserts and sweets, adapted to the client's
preferred theme.
Tools
Adobe Photoshop
Adobe Illustrator
Figma
Role
Sole Designer
Challenge
Creating a brand that differentiates itself and
resonates with customers.

DecoBap was ready for a brand experience that
positioned them as a business.

Process
Defining the brand: For this, I had to work with
the client to determine what their brand truly stands
for, and look at the skills and expertise that they
possess and made them stand out. It is also necessary
to know what DecoBap stands for and what are
their brand values.

Strategy: DecoBap identified its products as
home-based, high-quality, and rich in flavors. They
wanted to deliver the best possible product, and win
over customers like love at first sight. Thus, to be
able to establish the brand I decided to go with vivid
colors and a logo that will forever be associated with
the brand. Using red and yellows which are the chief
food colors stimulating the appetite as well as purple
for a pop of color that would make the brand grab
attention and stand out.
Color Palette
Typography
Why Castoro?
For Decobap we settle on a typeface that was easy to
remember and indicative of some aspect of the brand design.
Logos
For the logo, we decided on a cake since it was the
main product sold thus it can be easily understood
and recognized, but we wanted it to be more organic
and with movement therefore I added a drip glaze that
signified the decoration (referencing the brand name
deco) and some ribbons to represent a gift that
symbolizes love and devotion between DecoBap and
its customers.
