Spot Me

Description
Spot Me is an app prototype that helps to reunite
lost animals with their owners and assists stray
animals have a better quality of life, reducing
their time on the streets.
Tools
Proto.io
Adobe Illustrator
Role
Product Designer
Responsabilities
Prototype Design, Co-Survey Creation, and Design Decisions
Process
Brief: There are over 70 million animals
on the streets, these animals need to find their
owners and a home. We created a product to help
them by connecting people with technology. Spot Me
is an innovative app that helps to reunite lost
animals with their owners and helps stray animals
have a better quality of life. Essentially, we
created an easy way to find lost animals and help
those who don’t have where to live, reducing their
time on the streets.

Mockup: I designed the front-end user interface
using proto.io. To make an engaging user-friendly
interface I created different images, illustrations,
graphics, and arrangements including the app’s logo
and app currency using Adobe Illustrator.
Functionalities:
  • Report the location of stray animals
  • Create a profile for the lost pet
  • Receive automatic notifications every
    time a reported animal matches the
    characteristics of the pet
Challenge: How will the user be able to donate
to various charity groups and animal shelters?

Solution: Spot Me provides a platform for users
to donate to and receive more information on Animal
Rights Organizations, by gaining Paw Power, the
special in-app currency used to donate to
various groups.
Screens
Color Palette
Why?: I chose turquoise because it is a calm,
friendly, and cheerful color, that radiates the
tranquility of blue and the growth of green.
Then, I combined it with the vitality of yellow.
Competitors
Why choose Spot Me?
  • Competitors do not have a method to
    support local charities and businesses
  • Because Spot Me will be completely free
    to users. Our free service will provide our
    strongest competitive advantage in the market
  • Reporting methods rely solely on inter-user
    interractions, allowing a huge opening for
    dangerous activity
  • Because Spot Me partners with animal rights
    organizations and places a high emphasis on
    community values, our service will stand out in
    comparison to other apps or services in the market
Survey
We used a Survey as our methodology of research.
The essence of survey method is questioning
individuals on a topic, in this case stray
animals, and describing their responses.
The survey method was used in order to study
a variety of basic and applied questions to
establish the attitude of people towards stray
animals and our app.
