Price On Our Lives

Objective
Make a graphic system based on a social movement
with the goal of creating a flexible system that
can become a toolkit for social communication with
the speed we’ve become accustomed to today.
Tools
Adobe Photoshop
Adobe Illustrator
Adobe InDesign
Role
Sole Designer
Challenge
How big or small does a system have to be to
create a familiar mark that a reader can—almost
instantaneously—know who is speaking?
Process
Selecting a movement:

For this, I chose "Price on Our Lives" a movement
from the March for Our Lives a movement that
fights to end gun violence, the leading cause of
death for young people in America.

Strategy:

  • I avoided using Red and Blue so there was
    not a specific political wing associated
    with the movement
  • I kept the information clear and concise so
    it would grab the attention of the audience
Color Palette
I chose yellow because it is young, and grabs attention
