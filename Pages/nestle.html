Nestle Package Redesign

Description
We consider how to assess the quality of the
Nestle package design, and we made informed design
decisions to redesign both the product package
(the box itself) and the logo of the company.
Challenge
How does the customer view the brand? Does it meet
their expectations? Does it fulfill their needs on a
functional level and/or on an emotional level? How do
customers interact with and "experience" the brand?
Tools
Adobe Photoshop
Adobe Illustrator
Role
Co-Designer
Process
Redesign considerations:

  • Assessing good and bad design elements
  • The client (the company that makes the product) and
    the consumer (person or community who buys the product)
  • The Color scheme
  • Composition
  • Typography
  • Logos and branding
  • Creating a character
