Tea Packaging

Objective
I designed a functional package that can hold the
product, be transported and stored, and stands out.
The collection was designed to transmit clear,
clean, and calming energy. Creating a series
allowed me to reinforce the package design idea
as well as create a more attractive display.
Tools
Adobe Photoshop
Adobe Illustrator
Adobe InDesign
Role
Sole Designer
Process
Challenge:

Would people want to buy this? What do
you want to transmit? What makes a good
package design?

Strategy:

For this project, I decided to use pastel colors
to give the packages a feel of lightness,
softness, and calmness. This matches the product,
it gives it a feeling of earthiness and
sophistication. The collection is made from flat
icons to focus on color and shape. Making the
illustrations flow between packages was a decision
made to give consistency, allowing the packages all
to look like a unified collection.
