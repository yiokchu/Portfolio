SPOT ME



Collaboration with Ronald Kroening and Bradley Washington


Spot Me is an app prototype that helps to reunite lost animals with their owners and assists stray animals have a better quality of life, reducing their time on the streets.

I designed the front-end user interface using proto.io. To make an engaging user-friendly interface I created different images, illustrations, graphics, and arrangements including the app’s logo.


Functionalities:



  1. Report the location of stray animals
  2. Create a profile for the lost pet
  3. Receive automatic notifications every time a reported animal matches the characteristics of the pet
  4. Contribute to local businesses, animal rights groups, and shelters
  5. Provides a platform for users to donate to and receive more information on Animal Rights Organizations, by gaining Paw Power, the special in-app currency used to donate to various groups
